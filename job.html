Senior Graphic designer

Job ID : 2456
Banglore
Posted: November 21, 2021

Job details

What you’ll do
  • Study design briefs and determine requirements
  • Conceptualize visuals based on requirements
  • Prepare rough drafts and present ideas
  • Develop illustrations, logos, and other designs using software
  • Use the appropriate colors and layouts for each graphic
  • Work with copywriters and creative director to produce the final design
  • Ensure final graphics and layouts are visually appealing and on-brand
What we’re looking for
  • 3+ Years of experience in Graphic Design
  • Knows Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator. Knowledge of After Effects for Motion Design is a plus.
  • Ability to work independently and complete assigned tasks within identified time frames
  • Organized, dependable & detail-oriented
  • Team Player, Quick Learner & Efficient
  • Able to work well under pressure
  • Submission of sample work portfolio is mandatory.
